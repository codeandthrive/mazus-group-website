About Us

Mazus Group: Our Journey Towards Sustainability

Who We Are?

Mazus Group excels in two complementary sectors: Builders and Developers, and Hotels and Resorts. With a relentless pursuit of quality and customer satisfaction, we've become a recognized leader in real estate and hospitality. Our core values revolve around creating properties that coexist harmoniously with nature, uphold the ecosystem, and advocate for responsible development. We're dedicated to crafting exceptional, sustainable properties that set new industry standards while preserving our environment. Explore how Mazus Group is reshaping real estate and hospitality while staying true to our core values.



What we do?

At Mazus Group, we specialize in sustainable real estate development and exceptional hospitality, creating eco-friendly communities and delivering world-class guest experiences.



Chairman's Message

I am pleased to extend a warm welcome to you on behalf of Mazus Group. Our journey, which commenced on September 7th, 2018, traversing through good and bad times, has been nothing short of remarkable. Mazus Group stands as a beacon of innovation and excellence in the hospitality, tourism, and Builders and Developers industry. Our mission is to seamlessly integrate nature's marvels with our properties worldwide, creating lasting experiences for our guests, partners, and investors. Our vision extends beyond geographical boundaries, aspiring to establish an enduring legacy. From the humble Mazus Inn to the picturesque Park Place Holiday Home and the renowned Adithya Nature Resort & Spa, our journey reflects our commitment to excellence and growth. The Turkish-Mexican fusion cafe, the architectural marvel Swiss Holidays, and the upcoming Mazus World Tribal Museum showcase our dedication to innovation. Looking forward, our vision propels us beyond limits. Our expansion into Coorg, Karnataka State, symbolizes our confidence to explore new territories and provide unique experiences. It's not just business expansion; it's a commitment to unforgettable moments in one of India's most serene destinations. As we continue to redefine the boundaries of hospitality, tourism, and cultural preservation, I invite you to join us on this exciting journey. Thank you for being an integral part of the Mazus Group community.

Best Regards

Vision

At Mazus Group, our vision is to be a trailblazer in the construction and hospitality sectors, setting new standards for sustainable and eco-friendly development. We strive to create properties that not only provide world-class experiences to our guests but also promote environmental conservation, contributing positively to the communities we serve.



Mission

  • Building Sustainable Dreams: Our mission is to design and construct exceptional properties that blend seamlessly with their natural surroundings. We aim to develop sustainable communities that enhance the lives of our residents, ensuring minimal impact on the environment.

  • Exceeding Guest Expectations: As a hospitality pioneer, our mission is to deliver unparalleled guest experiences at Mazus Hotels & Resorts. We aim to create unforgettable moments for our guests while promoting cultural appreciation and preserving the local heritage.

  • Empowering Partnerships: Mazus Hotels & Resorts seeks to form mutually beneficial partnerships with other builders and groups, sharing our expertise to manage properties efficiently and profitably while upholding our commitment to ecological stewardship.
